Who is Nicolas?

I’m a kind, intelligent person who works really hard in my studies to avoid getting into trouble at home. I’m extremely loyal to my friends and always willing to help others. I believe people have the right to express themselves however they want, and no one has the right to say otherwise or to judge whether they agree or not. You know? Just let people be themselves. I was basically raised by a family of women because of a difficult relationship with my dad. So my biggest role model for strength and love is my mom. Even during a period of unemployment, she never let us go without a simple meal of rice and beans on the table. Oh, and I like kittens too. That’s it. S2
